What is NgColombia?

The Conference The Workshops Food & Fun

NgColombia 2017 is Colombia’s first Angular international conference. Held in Medellín, it gathers people from different parts of world to bring you an out-of-this-world experience. In this full-day we will dive into exciting topics such as RxJS, Angular CLI, TypeScript, AOT, Animations and much more!

NgColombia is split in two sections: in the morning we will have a set of 3 concurrent workshops and in the afternoon the main track with our keynotes. The talks and workshops will be mostly about Angular, with the primary focus on Angular 2 and later.

We know how important it is for you to have a real hands-on experience when you are learning Angular. That’s why we are bringing world top professionals with premium workshops just for you. During these sessions you will go through deep technical concepts and specialized content that will make you a real Angular Rockstar!

NgColombia is brought to you from another universe, one where food is delicious, coffee is awesome and having fun is the only rule. NgColombia has a lot, A LOT, of surprises for you.

Get ready, because this is about to start!
